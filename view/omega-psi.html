 In 2021 I had an idea for a new way of organizing some of the knowledge I've been gaining over the years; primarily, concerning mathematics, physics, and a touch of a few other subjects. Since I love studying and seeing progress of my studies, but also found my habit of taking "organic" notes in physical notebooks a bit too laborous and difficult to manage, I came up with a system to instead do this in Google Docs! This is the omega Notes project.

 You can find the links to the Table of Contents of the project and the introductory Notebook Zero in the navigation buttions above.

 psi Notes is my plan to now move away from Google Docs into a self-hosted static website generator, without abandoning the comfort of being able to write those notes with the help of Google Docs. Yes, it's another software development project on my backburner. I like having things to do!
